Recipe for Cream Cheese Penguins

Ingredients for 1 portion

  • 18 jumbo black olives, pitted
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 18 small black olives
  • 1 carrot

Steps

  1. Cut a slit lengthwise (from top to bottom) into one side of each jumbo olive.

    Carefully insert about 1 teaspoon cream cheese into slits in each jumbo olive.

  2. Slice carrot into 18 1/4-inch-thick rounds; cut a small (pie-shaped) notch out of each carrot slice to form feet.

    Save the cut out pieces; press pieces into centers of small olives to form beaks. If necessary cut a small slit into each small olive before inserting the beak.

  3. Set a jumbo olive, large hole side down, onto a carrot slice.

    Then, set a small olive onto the jumbo olive, adjusting so that the beak, cream cheese chest, and notch in the carrot (feet) slice line up. Secure with a toothpick.
